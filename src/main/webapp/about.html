About Us

SweatMate is a platform created by us, Arthur and Felix, two students from HTBLA Kaindorf.
As fitness enthusiasts and students, we embarked on this project with a shared passion for health and well-being.

With SweatMate, our aim was to develop a platform that allows users to upload and discover a variety of training plans.
We wanted to create a space where individuals could find workout routines tailored to their preferences and goals.

Through hard work and dedication, we brought SweatMate to life as a project at school.
It has been an exciting journey, from coding and designing to refining the platform's features.
